About Me.

I first got into development after high school, thinking I was good with computers (mainly because I played video games). However, I quickly realized I didn’t know much about programming itself. I struggled with basic algorithm problems early on, but I was determined to learn. I spent countless nights understanding the fundamentals—things like loops and variables—and eventually got hooked on coding. Over time, I found my passion in software development rather than algorithmic challenges, and that's when everything clicked.

During my first year studying computer science, I built a simple chess game in Python, and despite the messy code filled with hundreds of "if" conditions, I was immensely proud when it worked! That project marked the beginning of my journey as a software developer. Since then, I’ve worked on various projects, ranging from small games and CRUD applications to mobile apps and cross-platform solutions. My proudest achievement was fully developing a SaaS product, handling the entire development process from coding to testing and deployment across multiple environments.

I hold a Master’s in Software Engineering from Université Toulouse III - Paul Sabatier, and have had the chance to work with great companies like Sopra Steria as a full-stack intern and Thales , where I’m currently working as a Software and DevOps Engineer Apprentice in the Air Traffic Management sector

I'm actively seeking new opportunities to continue developing my skills as a software engineer while contributing to a team that’s passionate about creating innovative solutions. If you think I’d be a good fit, let’s connect and discuss how I can add value to your team!
